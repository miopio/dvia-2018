Natural and human-made seismic events in the last 30 days

Earthquake magnitude is a measure of the size of an earthquake at its source. It is a logarithmic measure. At the same distance from the earthquake, an increase of magnitude of 1.0 on the scale means an amplitude of the seismic waves approximately 10 times as large.

Not every seismic event is of natural origin. This dataset differentiates between earthquakes and quarry blasts & explosions. But there is also a third category of human induced earthquakes caused by human activity that alters the stresses and strains on the Earth’s crust.
