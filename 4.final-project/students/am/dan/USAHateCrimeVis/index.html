USA Hate Crime

by Dan Ran, Dec.11, 2018

This is an interactive data visualization of hate crime statistic in United States of America from 2015 to 2017.  Each bubble represents a number of hate crime incidents and different bias motivation reported by FBI. Bigger bubbles represent larger number of incidents. There are three layers of this project. By Changing the views, you can access the total number of incidents, the number of incidents by bias motivation, and a breakdown of each bias motivation. 

Data Source: United States Department of Justice, Federal Bureau of Investigation.(November 2018). Hate Crime Statistics
